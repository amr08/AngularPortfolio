A little about myself

In February of 2016, I embarked on a journey to change careers from the counseling profession to web development. As a self-taught coder, I worked independently for 5 months taking a combination of in-person classes with the group Girl Develop It (a group organized to promote women in technology), attended workshops, and completed online classes. Once I had my son that May, I knew I needed to be fully immersed into coding (outside of the house) if I were to ever become proficient in JavaScript and beyond. This is when I entered into the University of North Carolina's Full-Stack Web Development program and never looked back! I love what I do and look forward to continuing the expansion of my knowledge and experience.
